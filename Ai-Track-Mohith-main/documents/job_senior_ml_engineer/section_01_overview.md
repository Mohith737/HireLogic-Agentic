# Senior ML Engineer

## Role Summary
Own end-to-end machine learning systems from experimentation through production deployment. Partner with product, platform, and data teams to translate ambiguous business problems into measurable model outcomes.

## Business Context
- Product surfaces serve high-volume recommendation and ranking workloads.
- Candidate is expected to improve model quality while maintaining strict latency and reliability targets.
- Success requires balancing experimentation, engineering rigor, documentation quality, and cross-functional collaboration.

## Scope
- Build and productionize ML pipelines.
- Improve model serving performance and observability.
- Contribute to architecture reviews for large-scale ML systems.
- Communicate tradeoffs clearly to engineering and product stakeholders.
